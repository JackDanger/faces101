jQuery lets you change a web page

Try clicking all of the examples. Then look at the source to this page to discover why it works.

Fade out

This whole section will fade out when you click anywhere on it. Refresh the page to get it back

      $("#first_square").click(function(){
        $(this).fadeOut();
      })

Fade in

Something new will fade in when you click this.

      $("#second_square").click(function(){
        $("#fuzzy_kitten").fadeToggle();
      })

Slide around

This will slide up or down when you click it. Refresh the page to get it back

      $("#third_square").click(function(){
        $("#cute_puppies").slideToggle();
      })
